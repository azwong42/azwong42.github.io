Walkthrough Info for the Vantage™ Productivity Portal

What am I supposed to be doing?
The most important thing to know is that you can log out of the account using the "Log Out" button in the upper right (hidden in the ID card menu, which is accessed by clicking the circle with the AH in it). This will allow you to view achievement names, which are little jokes or puns that should hopefully guide you toward the action required to unlock the achievement.

Note also that completing a relevant action does not always award an achievement immediately--if you think you should be earning an achievement but aren't, try waiting about 15 seconds before pressing "log out". Not everyone responds immediately!

Why are some of the achievements obscured?
You probably don't have the ability to complete those yet. Most of the achievements require completion of "I'm In"-- once you've done that, it should become fairly obvious why.

Just walk me through the achievements.
Here's a full list of achievements. Hover over the black boxes to reveal descriptions and detailed instructions.
  1. Touch Grass - Log out of the account. - The "Log Out" button is hidden in the ID card menu, which is accessed by clicking the circle with the AH in it.
  2. I'm In - Enter the master password. - Evelyn's birthday is in Alice's calendar. Her age is in the event description.
  3. It's the Final Notice - Receive the seventh Productivity Notice. - The Productivity Notices are sent every 24 hours, at 11:07 AM, if Alice has not completed any tasks since the last notice.
  4. Reading Junkie - Read all of the junk emails. - Read an email by clicking on it. After you've read the fourth, wait a bit for an update.
  5. Taskmaster I - Check off a task before the seventh Productivity Notice. - Click a task to complete it. The Productivity Notices are sent every 24 hours, at 11:07 AM, if Alice has not completed any tasks since the last notice.
  6. Taskmaster II - Check off all tasks on the TODO list. - Click a task to complete it. After you've completed all of them, wait a bit for an update.
  7. Do Not Go Gentle - Send the notice of resignation. - Just click send--you can't edit the email. Afterwards, wait a bit for an update.
  8. Quelled Feelings - Delete the notice of resignation. - Just click delete.
  9. Howe-To - Send an email to Evelyn. - You can't compose emails on your own, so find one of Evelyn's and reply to it. Afterwards, wait a bit for an update.
  10. Mixed Message - Mark a junk email as important. - Just click the button.
  11. Artificial Unintelligence - Send an email to the NEXUS VPA. - You can't compose emails on your own, so find one of the NEXUS VPA's and reply to it. Afterwards, wait a bit for an update.
